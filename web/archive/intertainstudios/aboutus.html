John Lund graduated from the University of Southern California with a degree in philosophy and was in the second inaugural class of the Interactive Media Program, within the School of Cinematic Arts. After school he began operating a online retail store with a strong presence on the eBay as well as on the web. John dabbled in Web 2.0 while working for Boxador in Los Angeles, researching and analyzing emerging trends in the Web 2.0 marketplace. After that he landed a gig in the Bay Area working as lead interactive designer for a company called Virtual Space Entertainment. At VSE, John lead and worked on projects ranging from fullly 3D virtual environments for National Geographic to complex Flash interactives for the Smithsonian Institute, as well as developing a new standard in immersive retail shopping for the National Association of College Stores. Bringing this diverse experience to Intertain Studios, John works as the lead designer and owner to guide the company into the future.

Thomas Zukowski grew up in Boulder, Colorado and graduated with a degree in film production from USC's School of Cinematic Arts. Following his studies, Thomas worked in the art department of various film & television productions where he developed a strong interest in graphic design. Since finishing school, Thomas has made graphics for a long list of productions including Medium, Bones, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, and Monk. Most recently, he has moved his efforts into the field of web design where his love of the visual arts continues to hold influence. In his free time he enjoys playing baseball, creating electronic music, and rocking out in general.
