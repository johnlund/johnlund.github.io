Freedom


The seagulls soar overhead
Screaming with delight as they dive.
Sweet, cool, ocean air under their wings;
He finds a perch.

No longer a part of the group–
A lone traveler on a distant beach.
White foam sprays all around,
But he doesn’t frown for a thing.

The world is bright and new,
    Exciting and blue.
A seagull wonders if there is anything more
He should be doing before he explores.

Forget that and forget them.
The world is mine.
The seagull takes off,
And soars...
