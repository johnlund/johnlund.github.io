Deliberate Days

March 1, 2011


Deliberate days doing nothing.
Deliberately dazed, disbelieving everything.

An image comes to mind–lost in reality–
found again in the dark, a day-dream.

Without meaning or purpose–lost in reality–
life inches and crashes, a night-dream.

A boat without anchor feels the sweeping bliss of freedom.
A thought without form knows the wasted tragedy of desire.
